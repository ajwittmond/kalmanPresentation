\section{The Filtering Problem}
\begin{frame}

  Classically, time dependent systems are modeled by:

  \only<2>{
  Differential equations in the continuous case
  \begin{align}
    f &: \R^n \times \R \to \R^n \\
    \frac{dx}{dt} &= f(x,t)
  \end{align}

  Solved by:
  \begin{align}
    x : \R \to \R^n
  \end{align}
}
  \only<3>{

    Difference equations in the discrete case

    \begin{align}
      f &: \Z \times \R \to \R^n \\
      x_t &= f(x_{t-1},t)
    \end{align}

    Solved by:
    \begin{align}
      \{ x_t \}_{t\in \Z}
    \end{align}
    }
  \end{frame}
